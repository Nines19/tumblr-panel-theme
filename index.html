This Is The Sidebar

You can put your page navigation as a list of links, like this:

Navigation:

  • Home
  • About
  • Contact Us

Or anything else you want over here. Maybe a bit of quick information about your website and its purpose, or your site's latest updates.

The sidebar is also a great place for any Twitter or Facebook widgets. This theme's sidebar is 150 pixels wide, so you'll want to specify something smaller than that. (120 pixels is a good width.)
